About Me

I am a third year Ph.D. Candidate in ICT (Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences) During the past period in ICT, I gained helpful and rich experience of traditional network, cloud computing and software defined network. Currently, I’m interested in social network theories, machine learning algorithms and trajecory data mining.
