About Me

I am currently a fourth-year Ph.D. Student in Institute of Computing Technology(ICT), Chinese Academy of Sciences. My research interest lies in spatio-temporal data mining, text mining and deep learning. During the past period in ICT, I gained helpful experience on text mining, trajectory analysis and machine learning.
